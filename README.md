# GeniePod

![GeniePod hero](/assets/geniepod-hero.png)

GeniePod is open source home AI built to live where it belongs: in your home, not someone else's cloud.

OpenClaw proved something important: people do not just want access to AI. They want AI that feels personal, remembers context, and can be shaped around their own lives.

That desire is real. It arrived at exactly the right time, and it explains why the first wave of personal AI products resonated so strongly. But the first wave got the trust model wrong.

OpenClaw showed both the demand and the problem: people want personal AI, but household memory, routines, and conversations should not depend on remote providers.

GeniePod exists to fix that trust model. We believe the right place for home AI is in the home, that trust is an architectural decision, and that the more personal AI becomes, the more local it must become.

This is not a generic AI platform or a chatbot wrapped in hardware. It is home AI built for actual daily life: useful every day, trustworthy in shared space, and designed to be lived with.

**OpenClaw 🦞 proved the demand. GeniePod fixes the trust model.**
